Introduction

We will be organising few extra-curricular activities on every saturday that will help in the overall development of the child. Activities will include drawing, painting, speech, story telling, games etc.

We will also be conducting few visits for the childrens to malls and historical sites so that children can have bigger horizons, they can dream bigger and get motivated to work for a better living.

We will also be organising some festival celebration to teach children about importance of festivals.
