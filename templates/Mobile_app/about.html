Development of Rural Education in India


We have come up with an idea of turning buses into mobile schools, for the children belonging to the remote areas and who cannot go to school because in the mornings they are busy earning their livelihoods and also for those girls who are denied from educating themselves because of their family's security concerns. These buses are equipped with a digital screen and the students are taught through interactive videos and quizes. We have seen many videos surfacing on the social media where the teachers are seen reciting incorrect spellings. Teaching through structured and pre-recorded, self-explainatory videos based on NCERT eliminates the problem, as even a teacher with no or basic knowledge can now correct and educate themselves and then teach the students, giving rise to local employment at the same time. Moreover this video based learning will keep the students intact. The areas which do not have interet connectivity can run the website through satellite.
According to a survey,
68% of educators believe that video content stimulates discussions.
a. 66% believe video increases student motivation and is preferred by students.
b. 62% believe video helps teachers be more effective.
These headquarter will serve the purpose of their central examination centres, with external invigilators and evaluators to ensure that no cheating takes place unlike what we know of most of the rural schools and the quality of education is at par with city schools. The buses will be air conditioned, have biometric attendance system to monitor the progress and maintain an authentic database. These buses will start from 6 in the morning to 9 in the evening, taking 4 hour classes in 3 shifts in several areas; to ensure that working children do not miss their schooling. Also, conducting schools at the door-step and appointment of female staff predominantly will make sure that no girl is barred from schooling due to local goons, unsafe routes and fear of harassment. We will also have monthly parents teacher meeting to ensure that the children are growing in a positive atmosphere with a healthy mindset, lifestyle and values. The parents will be given regular feedback, via SMS service. It will inspire them to work towards achieving these goals rather than look to the daily stuggles they are going through.
